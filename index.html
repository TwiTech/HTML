Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from our application, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@yourapp.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, include your user ID and any other relevant information to identify your account.
  3. Our support team will process your request within 7 business days and confirm the deletion of your data.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team at support@yourapp.com.
